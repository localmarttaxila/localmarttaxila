Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Information Collection

When you place an order on Local Mart, we collect your name, Taxila area address, and order details. This is necessary to deliver your items from the local shops.

2. Data Usage

Your data is sent directly to the shop owner and the administrator via Formspree. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Security

Since this is a static site project, we do not store passwords. Payment is handled externally via JazzCash/EasyPaisa to ensure your financial safety.

4. Consent

By using this website, you consent to our collection of the order details you provide in the forms.
